A Vibrant Ecosystem Of Community Tools And Support.

  • Editors and IDE's

    SproutCore developers use a variety of editors, including Textmate, VIM and Emacs. For a more tailored experience, check out the SproutCore Textmate Bundle.

  • Hosting

    SproutCore apps build to a directory of static assets that can be deployed to any server. Hosting providers specifically targeting HTML5 apps will likely be best suited for SproutCore apps.

  • Support

    SproutCore support is currently offered by Strobe which employs several members of the SproutCore Core Team.

  • Consulting

    There are a number of major consulting firms building applications for Fortune 500 clients using SproutCore. Appnovation, RivetLogic and Rothbury Software are among them.
